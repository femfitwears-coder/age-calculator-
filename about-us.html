About Us

Who we are and what we build

Free Text Tools is a privacy-focused platform built by GrowthSolutionsDesk. Our goal is to provide fast, simple, and reliable online utilities that help users work better with text and calculations.

We believe useful tools should be accessible to everyone without requiring accounts, subscriptions, or personal data.

What We Offer

Our tools include word counters, age calculators, and other text utilities designed for students, writers, professionals, and everyday users.
